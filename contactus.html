Aquaway CSR Activity

Being at the forefront of innovation AQUAWAY team feels itself obliged to provide safe and pure drinking water to the underprivileged children in remote villages. With this in mind AQUAWAY has donated the first of its kind solar powered mobile water purifiers to a ZillaParishad High schools in the remote villages of Chittoor Dist & Nalgonda Dist. Apart from primarily focussing on providing safe and pure drinking water with this unit which runs exclusively on solar energy, the secondary aim is to kindle the spirit of innovation among the students at a tender age. Till date we have adopted 5 schools with the same mission. We intend to expand this charitable work as we grow.
